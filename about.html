Our Specialities

Meenakshi Maternity & Women care Centre is a 16 bedded Mother & Child Care Hospital established with the aim to make motherhood a memorable experience. The specialized center is supported by reputed doctors of various specialities and also has a basic level neonatology unit. The hospital has exceptional expertise in high risk pregnancy, pre term delivery, gynae-lap surgery, pediatric health and adolescent care.

The aim of the hospital is to provide – More for Motherhood, and offer comprehensive care services for every woman. It is the first hospital of its kind in the region, providing specialized programs to serve the needs of a new mother, infant and an adolescent.

Meenakshi Maternity & Women care Centre is a pioneer in introducing programs developed to address the apprehensions, fears and problems of mothers. The programs developed with keen clinical insight and supported by ancillary services provide perinatal solutions. From lactation counseling, mommy massage, toddler/infant care to ante natal classes, post-delivery weight reduction program, stretch mark treatment and adolescent care program, the hospital aims at providing comprehensive medical and supportive care to the community.

Meenakshi Maternity & Women care Centre also has state of the art ICU, level- III NICU and modular operation theaters to treat all gynae, pediatric & neonatal complications. Moreover the hospital is also supported by an ACLS Ambulance to transfer patients opting for 24X7 emergency cares.

The Specialised Centre is Supported by reputed doctors of Various specialities and also has a basic level of neonatology unit.

The hospital also has specialized dedicated institutes For Cosmetology, Dentistry and Physiotherapy.

We conduct regularly antenatal Counselling Sessions on Maternal Nutrition,Food Habits and Excercise during Pregnancy and also Counselling on Breast Feeding.

24/7 Maternity Care:
We have 24*7 Maternity Service,with Experienced Obstetrician and well trained Staff and well equipped Air Conditioned Labour Room with continuous Fetal Monitoring and Provisions for epidural Anesthesia(Painless Labour).

Pediatrics Care:
We have basic level of Neonatal Intensive Care(NICU) with well tranied staff and experienced paediatricians. Facilities for Preterm baby care and Phototherapy available. Along with providing exceptional medical expertise, Meenakshi Maternity & Women care Centre is also instrumental in initiating numerous patient engagement and awareness programs that aim at empowering the women of today to make the right decisions for a health tomorrow.
