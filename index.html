Maternity & Women Wellness

Meenakshi Maternity & Women care Centre is a 16 bedded Mother & Child Care Hospital established with the aim to make motherhood a memorable experience. The specialized center is supported by doctors of national and international repute and also has a nationalized neonatology unit. The hospital has exceptional expertise in high risk pregnancy, pre term delivery, gynae-lap surgery, pediatric health and adolescent care.
